About Us

One step closer to healthier living

Quantum Medical Supply is a nationwide durable medical equipment provider on a mission to make getting essential supplies easier for patients living with diabetes and lymphedema.

Our story

Quantum Medical Supply was founded with a simple belief: patients shouldn't have to fight through paperwork and red tape to get the medical supplies their doctors prescribe. Founded by a Board-Certified Pedorthist, we started by helping people receive properly fitted therapeutic shoes from qualified professionals — and grew from there.

Today we serve patients nationwide, helping them access diabetic shoes, compression garments, and continuous glucose monitors through Medicare and commercial insurance. We take on the bulk of payer coordination, appeals, and documentation so patients and providers don't have to.

What we believe

Getting the right supplies should be straightforward, affordable, and respectful of your time. We measure our success by how easy we make the experience — from the first eligibility check to every reorder that follows.

What sets us apart

  • Specialist expertise in therapeutic footwear and DME.
  • Insurance navigation across Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payers.
  • Nationwide reach with home delivery.
  • Ongoing support with proactive reorders and renewals.
