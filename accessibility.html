Accessibility Statement

We want everyone to be able to use our website with ease.

Quantum Medical Supply is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and to continually improve the experience for all visitors.

What we do

  • Use clear, readable text with sufficient color contrast.
  • Provide descriptive text alternatives for meaningful images and icons.
  • Support keyboard navigation and visible focus indicators.
  • Structure pages with proper headings and landmarks for assistive technologies.
  • Respect "reduced motion" preferences in your browser or device.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our site and welcome feedback that helps us improve.
