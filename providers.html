The Referral Process

Simple to start, easy to manage

Send us a referral and we take it from there — keeping you informed at each step.

  • Submit the referral. Indicate the products needed (shoes, compression, CGM) and the patient's information.
  • We verify benefits with the patient's payer and determine coverage and eligibility.
  • We gather documentation, coordinating with your office for any prescription or chart notes required.
  • We deliver & maintain. Supplies ship to the patient, and we manage reorders and reauthorizations going forward.
