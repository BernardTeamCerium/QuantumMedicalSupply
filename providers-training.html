For Providers

In-house training for your team

We come to you. Quantum Medical offers complimentary, on-site training that helps your staff confidently identify candidates, take accurate measurements, and refer patients for diabetic shoes, compression garments, and continuous glucose monitors — with less back-and-forth and cleaner documentation.

Request Training Call (800) 704-6515
What We Cover

Practical training, tailored to your office

Sessions are hands-on and built around your team's day-to-day workflow. We focus on the things that reduce denials, save your staff time, and get patients their supplies faster.

Product knowledge

An overview of diabetic shoes & inserts, graduated compression garments, and CGM systems — including which patients are typically candidates and what each product does.

Fitting & measurement

Best practices for accurate sizing and measurement so garments and footwear fit correctly the first time, reducing returns and remakes.

Documentation & compliance

What payers look for — chart notes, prescriptions, and supporting documentation — so referrals move smoothly and meet coverage requirements.

Referral workflow

How to submit a referral, what we need from your office, and how we keep you informed at each step from verification to delivery.

Q&A with our specialists

Time for your team to ask questions about specific patients, coverage scenarios, products, and processes.

Flexible scheduling

In-person at your office or virtual, scheduled around your team — including lunch-and-learns and short refresher sessions.

Who It's For

Built for the whole care team

Whether you're a physician practice, podiatry or wound care clinic, or a busy front office, our training meets your team where they are.

  • Physicians & clinical staff — identify candidates and document with confidence.
  • Medical assistants & nurses — measurements, fitting basics, and patient education.
  • Front office & referral coordinators — a clean, simple referral process.
  • No cost to your practice — training is provided as part of our partnership.
