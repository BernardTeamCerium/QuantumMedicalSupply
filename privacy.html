Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Template notice: This is a starter privacy policy. Please have it reviewed and customized by qualified counsel to ensure it reflects your actual data practices and complies with applicable laws (including HIPAA, where relevant).

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, contact details, insurance information, and the products you're interested in, as well as information collected automatically when you use our website (such as basic analytics and device information).

How we use information

  • To verify insurance eligibility and coordinate your supplies.
  • To communicate with you about your requests, orders, and renewals.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

How we share information

We may share information with your insurance payer, your healthcare providers, and trusted service partners as needed to fulfill your requests, and as required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

Protected health information

Any protected health information (PHI) we handle is treated in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including HIPAA where it applies. Please do not send sensitive health information by email; we will collect any needed details through a secure process.

Your choices

You may contact us to access, update, or request deletion of your information, subject to legal and recordkeeping requirements.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at (561) 432-8200 or info@quantummedicalsupply.com.
