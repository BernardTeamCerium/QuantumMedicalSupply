Therapeutic Footwear

Diabetic Shoes & Inserts

Diabetic shoes are specially designed therapeutic footwear that help prevent complications for people living with diabetes. Prescribed for patients with foot concerns, they accommodate deformities, reduce pressure points, and minimize the risk of injury.

  • Custom and prefabricated insert options
  • Free fittings with qualified specialists
  • Designed to reduce pressure and protect at-risk feet
  • Often covered annually under Medicare's therapeutic shoe benefit
Check Eligibility

Who may qualify

Medicare may cover one pair of therapeutic shoes and inserts per calendar year for patients with diabetes who also have certain qualifying foot conditions, when prescribed by a physician. We'll confirm your specific eligibility.

Lymphedema & Swelling

Compression Garments

Compression garments are a practical, effective solution for managing lymphedema. They provide continuous, graduated support that helps reduce swelling, improve circulation, and support mobility throughout the day.

  • Garments for arms, legs, and more
  • Proper fitting for comfort and effectiveness
  • Renewal reminders so you always have fresh garments
  • Coverage available through many plans

Diabetes Management

Continuous Glucose Monitors

A continuous glucose monitor (CGM) is a small wearable device that tracks blood sugar levels throughout the day and night. Readings are sent in real time to a reader or smartphone, giving patients, caregivers, and providers actionable insight for better diabetes management.

  • Leading systems including Dexcom and FreeStyle Libre
  • Real-time readings without routine fingersticks
  • Automatic sensor reorders so you stay covered
  • Coverage available through Medicare and many plans
Check Eligibility

Who may qualify

CGM coverage is often available for patients managing diabetes who meet their plan's criteria, when prescribed by a physician. We'll verify your benefits and coordinate the prescription with your doctor.
